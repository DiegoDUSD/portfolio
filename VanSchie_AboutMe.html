Diego's About Me

This is the online portfolio of Diego van Schie. Diego is a programmer that has been worked with many technologies. His interest for computer programming started young. He has always loved to sit behind a computer and use it for anything from programming, playing video games, chatting with people across the world, and doing work.


Diego has shown himself to be able to be ambitious in what he wants to do. He has previously worked on both large and small projects like BattleMania, Simple Serialization, and Adaptable Configuration Language. When he was in the ninth grade in high school, he spoke with some of the developers of his school and helped solve security holes in their software. In addition, he worked with his father's company to help them design a CSS and JavaScript library named Image Knobs.


Diego has been programming for five years, and has built up most of his experience in Java. He has also worked with languages like JavaScript, Python, PHP, and some others. He has talked with people in commission work for experience in platforms like Minecraft and Roblox, which helped him learn about commission work.


Diego has been a programmer his whole life, and has always shown interest and willingness to participate in anything related to it. This site shows some of the work that he has done.
